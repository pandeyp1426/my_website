About Me

I’m Pradeep Pandey, a Computer Science major at the University of Wisconsin–Stout with a concentration in Mobile Application Development and a strong foundation in full-stack development. Over the past year I’ve gone beyond classroom projects and started building real applications — most notably RankMyStocks, a React + Flask + MySQL web app that combines API integration, UI/UX design, and database management. I’m also part of a collaborative “Exercise Bingo” system that applies gamification to help students stay active, where I contribute to both front-end logic and team coordination. My current technical skill set includes experience with C++, Python (Flask), Java, JavaScript, React, HTML/CSS/Tailwind, SQL/MySQL, Git/GitHub workflows, Agile development, and Linux-based tooling through VSCode and Ubuntu. I enjoy working with APIs, building responsive UIs, and structuring clean database designs, and I’m always looking for ways to refine performance, usability, and scalability. Alongside technical development, I bring strong leadership and communication skills from working as a Student Manager and Conference Assistant, roles where I learned how to manage teams, handle real-time problem solving, and maintain reliability in fast-paced environments. Community involvement is also important to me — through the Leo Club of Prayasnagar-Kathmandu and various volunteer initiatives, I’ve helped organize and contribute to service projects that create real social impact. I’m the type of developer who enjoys learning by building — experimenting with ideas, transforming concepts into working prototypes, and improving through iteration. With a solid math background (Linear Algebra, Calculus I–III, Discrete Math) and an interest in solving real-world problems through technology, I’m now working toward gaining internship or co-op experience where I can continue growing while contributing to meaningful projects.
